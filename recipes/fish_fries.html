Indian Fish Fries




Fish is packed with protein, vitamins, and nutrients that can lower blood pressure and help reduce the risk of a heart attack or stroke. Try this simple deep fried fish recipe to get the abundance of goodness with a delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • fish
  • salt
  • garam masala powder
  • turmeric powder
  • red chili powder
  • besan
  • ginger garlic paste

Steps:

  1. You can skip the marination if you are short on time.
  2. Coconut oil or mustard oil can be used for frying too.
  3. If you do not enjoy the crispiness in fish, then simply fry a for shorter amount of time.
  4. Seer fish, king fish, catla, barracuda, salmon can be used for preparing this recipe.
  5. Increase or decrease the amount of red chili powder to suit your taste. You can totally skip the chili powder if you are not fond of spicy food.
